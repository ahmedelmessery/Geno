Is a bioinformatics website helps students and researchers
to make Analysis , Detection and Prediction on biological data

Services

Bioinformatics tool to compute and analysis biological data, detect SNPs or mutation of some diseases and prediction genetic disorder

Prediction

Prediction on genetic disorder predict is the person will be affected by genetic disorder or not and the type of genetic disorder (single - gene disorder , chromosomal disorder , complex disorder) and its genetic subclass

Analysis

Responsible for population genetic analysis to compute summary statics including allele frequency and allele number .. etc provide computation of linkage disequilibrium statics shows some basics data quality checks for the markers and represented in the form is graph

Detection

Detects if SNPs ( single nucleotide polymorphism ) in a genome influence disease and the place of the SNP we have four diseases (Systemic Lupus Erythematosus - Multiple Sclerosis - Migraine - Epilepsy )
